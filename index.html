Get Started Submit

Who?

All Hack Clubbers! Anyone, anywhere HCB operates, who is 18 under! High Schoolers who are over 18 in the USA are also allowed to submit through a club / hackathon!

What?

Burgers is a YSWS! You are expected to make a full-stack web project that has a front-end website and a back-end program that integrates together to be something cool and fun!

In return we give you a grant to have some fullly stacked burgers!

See the requirements page for more information.

How?

Burgers is an YSWS event that is all about you making your own unique fullstack project!

Cool features can be found all over the internet, and it is encouraged for you to do research on specific attributes you may want to add, just remember to not copy-paste anything, and don't claim other's code as your own!

There is a guide dedicated to Burgers and it is recommended that you check it out!
